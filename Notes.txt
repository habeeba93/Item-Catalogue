- To commit to git
	Switch to "Team Explorer" (next to Solution Explorer)
	Click the "Home" icon.
	Click "Changes"
	Enter a message to descibe the commits being made
	Click "Commit All" once the button has been enabeled (after a message has been added)
	After commiting, click "Home" again and then "Sync"
	Click "Push" (should be enabaled if commits are present)
